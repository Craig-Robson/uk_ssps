UK SSPs - spatial data

UK SSP's datasets

This page makes available downloads for the UK SSPs which have been mapped making the outputs from the SSPs project available in various spatial formats at a range of spatial and (dis)aggregation levels. Links to the source datasets, including the outputs from the original SSPs project and the boundary layers used to map and derive spatial aggregate versions are also available.

These datasets have been generated by the OpenCLIM project which is looking to employ the UK SSPs within it's own work. As a result of out our work to we have therefore decided to share the spatial versions of the datasets, making them available to the wider community and complimenting the original files made available through the SSPs project.


We have made a number of datasets available, but this is not an extensive list, with more datasets being added over the coming months covering further SSP variables and different levels of disaggregation for all variables where possible.


Downloads

Population

Population data for the UK. The total population at decadal intervals for the 5 SSP scenarios from 2020 to 2100 using a 1km grid.

  • Population - .gpkg - .shp (.zip)
  • Population by scenario - .gpkg - .shp (.zip)

Demography

Demographic population breakdown. At Local Authority District resolution for the 5 SSP scenarios with 19 age classes.
  • Demography by scenario - .gpkg
  • Demography by scenario and age class - .gpkg (.zip)

Employment

Employment counts. Count of number of people employed in each of the 17 sectors for each year (from 2020-2100) at Local Authority District level for all five SSPs.
  • Employment by scenario - .gpkg
  • Employment by scenario and sector - .gpkg

Urbanisation

Development of land. Presents status of land development (an artificial surface) or not for the UK for all five SSPs at decadal intervals (2020-2100) on a 1km grid.
  • Urbanisation - .gpkg - .shp (.zip)
  • Urbanisation by scenario - .gpkg - .shp (.zip)

Original data

Original data (spreadsheet) as accessed and downloaded on 19th July 2021 from the SSPs project page.

  • Spreadsheet - .xls

Boundary datasets

Boundary datasets of which the SSP data can be mapped with. Datasets are presumed based on the available metadata with the SSP datasets.

  • 1km grid - .gpkg
  • Local authority districts (December 2020) - .gpkg
  • NUTS2
  • NUTS3
